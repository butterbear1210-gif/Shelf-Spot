Rumah Attap Library

A Hidden Gem Awaits

One thing I've realised throughout my ShelfSpot journey is that every library has its own personality. Some are known for their impressive architecture, while others stand out because of the people and stories behind them. That curiosity brought me to Rumah Attap Library And Collective, an independent community library located inside the historic Zhongshan Building. Unlike the public libraries and bookstores I've visited before, this space combines books, art, culture, and community, creating a reading experience that feels both meaningful and inspiring.

Almost There...

I kept telling myself, "Just a little bit more!" after climbing a few flights of stairs, I was finally about to reach the library. Considering how far I had travelled, I was really looking forward to exploring it. Unfortunately... my visit didn't quite go according to plan..

An Unexpected Turn!

Unfortunately, things didn't go quite as planned. Even though Google showed that the library was open when I checked before my visit, it happened to be closed when I arrived. Perhaps there was an unexpected reason that day. Although I couldn't explore the inside, I was still able to catch a glimpse of the library through its glass doors. Maybe I'll just have to save this one for another visit.

Although I was a little disappointed, I didn't want the trip to end just like that. Since we were already there, we decided to spend some time exploring the surroundings of the library instead. We walked around the staircase area, admired the old architecture of the building, and simply enjoyed the peaceful atmosphere before heading home. Sometimes, even when things don't go as planned, you can still find something worth appreciating.

This visit may not have gone as planned, but it certainly won't be my last. I'll definitely return to Rumah Attap Library, and when I do, I'll be sure to share the full experience with you. Stay tuned!
