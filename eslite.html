Eslite Bookstore

Where Books, Art And Lifestyle Meet

My visit to Eslite Spectrum wasn't actually planned. After watching a Muay Thai competition at Dataran Merdeka, I stumbled upon information about this beautiful bookstore in The Starhill, Bukit Bintang. It immediately caught my attention, so I decided to continue my little adventure by taking the MRT from Pasar Seni to Bukit Bintang. Before I knew it, I was standing right in front of one of the most talked-about bookstores in Kuala Lumpur!

Stepping Into Eslite

The adventure began the moment I stepped into The Starhill. To reach Eslite, I took the escalator up to Level 1. While going up, I couldn't help but admire the view from above! It already look so beautiful before I had even entered the bookstore! After that, I simply walked through the entrance, ready to explore every corner...

The destination!

My first impression? It felt more like a cultural space than a typical bookstore. Every corner was thoughtfully designed, and the combination of books, lifestyle products, and warm lighting made the place feel inviting from the start!

Stepping Inside!

To be honest, I wasn't sure where to look first.😂 Every corner seemed to have something interesting, from shelves filled with books to cosy reading spaces where people were quietly enjoying their own time. I even spotted a dedicated children's section, making the whole bookstore feel welcoming for visitors of all ages.

I think that's all for today! Thank you for visiting my web blog!⭐
